# DORA Metrics

The DORA program identifies four key metrics for software delivery performance. The four metrics are deployment frequency, lead time for changes, change failure rate, and time to restore service.

Elite performing teams deploy on demand, multiple times per day. For elite teams the lead time for changes is less than one hour. The change failure rate for elite performers is between zero and fifteen percent.

Time to restore service for elite teams is less than one hour. Research shows that the same practices improve both throughput and stability rather than forcing a trade-off between them.

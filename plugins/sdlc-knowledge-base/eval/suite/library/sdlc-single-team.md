# Single-Team SDLC Method

The single-team method is designed for one team of collaborating developers. It adds shared review and coordination practices on top of the solo method.

The single-team method requires that every change is reviewed by another team member before merge. It uses a shared backlog and a common definition of done so the team works to one standard.

The single-team method is the right default for most small to medium projects with one delivery team. Programmes that grow to multiple coordinated teams should adopt the programme method instead.

# Incident Response

An incident is an unplanned interruption or degradation of a service. The goal of incident response is to restore normal service operation as quickly as possible while minimizing impact on users.

The incident commander is the single person responsible for coordinating the response during an active incident. The incident commander delegates tasks, tracks status, and makes decisions, but does not necessarily fix the problem directly.

After an incident is resolved the team holds a blameless postmortem. A blameless postmortem focuses on the systemic causes of failure rather than blaming individuals, so that people share information openly.
